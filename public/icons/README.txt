This folder is used for manually creating and storing your Rush Royale overlay icons.

Place your PNG files here, named as follows:
- trophy.png          -> used for Total Trophies, Stream Gain, Season Gain
- sword.png           -> used for Total Crit
- wl.png              -> used for Stream W/L

Make sure each file:
- has a transparent background
- is around 128x128px (OBS scales well)
- follows the purple/gold Rush Royale style
